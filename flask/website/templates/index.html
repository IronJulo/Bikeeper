What is BiKeeper?

BiKeeper is a GPS tracker. BiKeeper device is connected everywhere and linked to our powerful software.
When we detect theft, a notification is automatically sent to the motorbike owner.

Localisation in real time

See were is your vehicle on a map directly in a web browser...

Real time notification

Be alerted when your vehicle is moved or shocked direclty on your phone.

Save your trip

See your daily trips done. Watch your stats, like speed, distance and more!

Alert your friends

When BiKeeper detect an accident, a message is send to your friends. They can take care of you.
