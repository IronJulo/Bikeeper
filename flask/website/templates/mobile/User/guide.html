How to install my device?

Locate your battery

STEP 1

The first step of the installation is to detect the position of your battery. Most of the time, it is located under your tank or your seat.

Connect the device

STEP 2

Once the battery is located, you will have to try to connect the device to the electrical poles of the battery.

First, connect the positive terminal which is usually red.

Then, do the same with the negative terminal which is often blue or black.

The hardest part is done!

Remove the adhesive

STEP 3

Gently pull the back tab to expose the adhesive portion of the device.

Affix the Device

STEP 4

Fix the device on a flat surface while respecting the direction of the affixing index

Your device is now installed! Congratulations!

Check for proper installation

STEP 5

Now that your device is installed, it is important to check that it is properly connected to your motorcycle. We therefore invite you to turn on the power and test your BiKeeper device right now.

Good road

the device is now fully ready to live great moments with you! Take care and see you soon!

How does my device work?

With BiKeeper you don't have to worry about missing features! We have made a list of all the things that could be useful for you in the future! If you have any questions, please contact us at the following email address: bikeeper.team@gmail.com

First, here are the sms you have to type to use your device :

SMSDescription
parkedTo activate the park mode on your device. It will switch to anti-theft mode and alert you if necessary.
unparkTo deactivate the park mode on your device. The system will no longer theft and vibration.
start The system will start to record your positions and make statistics of your trip. It will also detect vibrations and switch to protection mode.
stopThe system will no longer record your journey or protect you. It will also save your journey on your statistics page.
positionThe system will send you your device position. It is very useful to be sure your motorbike is safe.
