What is BiKeeper?

BiKeeper is a GPS tracker. BiKeeper is connected everywhere and linked to our powerfull software. When we detect theft, a notification is automaticaly sent to the motorbike owner.

How does it work?

Within your vehicle lies the Bikeeper device, his function is to read many kinds of data and then send them to you in your account. Bikeeper wants you to feel protected and assured therefore you can access to all of the details the device received (the state of your vehicle, and your last rides) and it will send notifications if something happens either to you or your vehicle. Furthermore, if you are connected and you have an accident, your device will automatically send a message to one of your contact.
